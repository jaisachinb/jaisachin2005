Bio

I am a final-year CS undergraduate at SRM Institute of Science and Technology (graduating May 2026), with research interests in usable privacy and security, human-computer interaction, and human-centered cybersecurity. I also have a secondary interest in human–AI interaction, misinformation, and the trust and safety of AI systems.

My work investigates how people perceive, reason about, and act on the security and privacy properties of digital systems — and how design can support safer human decisions. I am an incoming Research Intern at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (May 2026), working in the Usable Privacy & Security group. I also hold a concurrent Diploma in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras.
